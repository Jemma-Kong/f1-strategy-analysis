# F1 Strategy Analysis

Personal project exploring Formula 1 race strategy, energy management, 
and driver behavior through data analysis and simulation. Focus on the 
2026 hybrid power unit regulation changes (50% electric power, 350kW MGU-K).

## Motivation

The 2026 F1 regulations significantly increase the role of electric power 
in the power unit. Energy deployment strategy becomes a core determinant 
of lap time. This project investigates how optimal energy management 
strategies depend on individual driver styles.

## Roadmap
